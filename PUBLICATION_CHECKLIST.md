# MeetingToAction Publication Checklist

## Verdict

MeetingToAction is the "not recommended by HCL" bet. It is not a Top15 case, but
it is easy for judges to understand and demonstrates the same winning pattern:
document-first input, agent trace, cited evidence, human review and measurable
time saved.

- `python scripts/demo_smoke.py` passes.

sent or created."
